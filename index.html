I'm Jimmy, an outcome-focused Design Leader with over 10 years experience building products and teams that people genuinely love.

What I do

Currently, I'm part of the Design Leadership Team at Perk, leading a brilliant team of designers who are reimagining the future of business travel. I also play a leading role in shaping strategic product initiatives.

Previously, I led mobile design at Airwallex, taking their native app from 0-1. Before that, I led mobile and ecommerce design at AKQA in Melbourne, helping Australia's favourite retailer go from no online presence to $2million in daily sales in two years.

Recently, I left my dead phone in a taxi in Thailand, and realised how f**ked that scenario is. So, I created Foundit to help others in the same situation.
